Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Atech Engineering Solutions SRL ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Tetris Lumi ("the App") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Tetris Lumi is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be clear about what information we do and do not collect:

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Network information

2.2 Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • High scores and game statistics
  • Game settings and preferences (sound, haptics, display options)
  • Game progress and achievements

This data is stored using your device's local storage and can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing app data.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, share, or process any data about you. All game data remains on your device and is used solely to provide you with the game experience.

4. Third-Party Services

Tetris Lumi does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The App operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Tetris Lumi is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

6. Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, the security of this data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a secure lock screen
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Installing apps only from official app stores

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • To delete your high scores: Use the "Clear All Scores" button in the High Scores screen
  • To reset your settings: Use the "Reset to Defaults" button in the Settings screen
  • To delete all app data: Uninstall the App or clear app data from your device settings

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal information about you, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your data remains under your control on your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing notice within the App when appropriate

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. International Users

Tetris Lumi can be used internationally. Since no data is transmitted to our servers, there are no international data transfer concerns. All data remains on your local device.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

12. Audio and Haptic Features

The App includes optional sound effects and haptic feedback features. These features:

  • Run entirely on your device
  • Do not record or transmit any audio
  • Can be disabled at any time in Settings
  • Do not access your device's microphone

13. Permissions

Tetris Lumi requires minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your game settings and high scores locally on your device
  • Vibration: To provide haptic feedback during gameplay (optional, can be disabled)

The App does not request access to contacts, camera, microphone, location, or other sensitive device features.
